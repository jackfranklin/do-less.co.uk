Workshops for the efficient developer
Do-Less workshops are run by Jack Franklin and focus in on improving your developer workflows, processes and efficiency. By automating mundane tasks you can free your mind and focus for the more difficult, challenging problems that we all enjoy getting stuck into.

Building Web Applications with NodeJS and Express
In this workshop we'll dive into the world of NodeJS and build a simple web application in it.
